About

The AMV System Demo is a work-in-progress proof-of-concept prototype application.

Primary Goals

  • Create a system that displays mobility data as it flows through in real-time
  • Give insights for people keeping track of a fleet of moving objects
  • Finding possible improvements and help in understanding the usage and impacts of mobility services within a certain domain
  • Provide a small preview of the data available on the AMV Platform

Prototype Stage

This application is currently in a prototype stage. Be prepared to see some rough edges or under-construction features. During the ongoing development you may experience technical misbehaviour or small bugs here and there. You may also feel like some key features are missing - because they are. Feel free to suggest a feature you want to see in the near future - just open a feature request on GitHub.
